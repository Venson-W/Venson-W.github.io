This research develops efficient hybrid numerical frameworks for modeling fluid migration and fault-fluid interactions across scales. The work combines volume-based methods and boundary integral solutions to capture pore-pressure diffusion and elastic responses around fault structures without modeling an unnecessarily large bulk domain.
